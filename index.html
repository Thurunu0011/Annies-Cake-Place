ABOUT US

Welcome to Annie's Cake Place, where we create the most delicious cakes and cupcakes for any occasion. Our founder and owner, Manisha, is a talented baker with years of experience working in busy Melbourne restaurants, and she takes pride in using only the finest ingredients to ensure that every cake and cupcake is both beautiful and delicious.

At Annie's Cake Place, we believe that ordering a cake or cupcakes should be easy and stress-free. That's why we offer personalized service and handle all orders over the phone or through WhatsApp. Our team is dedicated to making the process as smooth as possible, from helping you choose the perfect flavors and designs to ensuring timely and professional delivery.

Whether you're celebrating a birthday, wedding, anniversary, or any other special occasion, we can work with you to create a cake or cupcake display that perfectly captures the spirit of the event. With a variety of flavors and designs to choose from, or the option to create a custom cake or cupcake just for you, our team is dedicated to making your cake-buying experience as enjoyable as possible.

All of our cakes and cupcakes are made to order, so you can be sure that your treats will be fresh and delicious when they arrive. We offer delivery options to make it easy for you to enjoy our cakes and cupcakes from the comfort of your own home. Simply place your order over the phone or through WhatsApp, and we'll take care of the rest.

Thank you for considering Annie's Cake Place for your next celebration. We are excited to work with you to create a cake or cupcake display that will exceed your expectations and make your occasion even more special.
